Usage:
\ffload filename\f7

Copies the specified file(s) into \fe/ram/cart\f7. \fffilename\f7 can be a file or a folder.
This clears out the previously loaded file(s).

If \fffilename\f7 starts with a #, then \ffload\f7 will attempt to download a cartridge from the BBS.
