\ffedit\f7 and \ffopen\f7 are equivalent

Usage:
\ffedit\f7/\ffopen\f7 filename\f7

The editor is chosen automatically based on the extension of the filename.
\f6Supported extensions:
\fa.lua .txt .pn .gfx .map .sfx .pod .theme\f7

If the filename is a folder or a .p64 file, then it will be shown in the file browser.
